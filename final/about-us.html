About Us

Chang's Garden offers delicious dining and takeout to Storrs, CT. Chang's Garden is a cornerstone in the Storrs community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
