I was driving around Storrs and asked a young man what the beast place to eat was. He told me Chang's. I went to Chang's, then. I have been going to Chang's ever since. The best Chinese food I have ever had, from Maine to Florida. I was driving 40 miles, once a week, you get Chang's food. Now, I have purchased my home in Storrs, I can eat there several times a week. Love the owner. She's just lovely. For the hours she puts in, I can't believe she always has that warm smile. And, she remembered my food order. Extremely attentive to customers.

-John

Always delightful. Not heavy but very flavorful. Had Egg Roll and Chicken with Cashews. Would order the same again in a flash. Ready as soon as I got there - Order was correct (I had specified pork fried rice with one order, white rice with the other). Correctly done.The food is fresh and flavorful. The takeout woman at the counter is friendly and courteous. Quantities and price are reasonable. Best Chinese in the area!

-Rob
